These are my favorite places to...

Eat!

  • Vesta (East Rutherford, NJ) - Amazing gourmet personal pizza!
  • Hiro Sushi (Lyndhurst, NJ) - Who doesn't love some great sushi?
  • Villa Mannino's (Bordentown, NJ) - The best pasta fagiola and great Italian dishes

Okay, enough of food. Now I'm hungry. But let's go onto my favorite small towns...

Stroll (Small Towns)

  • Princeton, NJ - Intellectual college town with world-class food and beautiful shops!
  • New Hope, PA / Lambertville, NJ - Straddling the Deleware river about 30 minutes north of Trenton, NJ lie these two quaint-but-eccentric towns. Be sure to grab a drink at one of the many bars or restaurants and catch some live music.
  • Bordentown City, NJ - Okay, this is a homer pick obviously, but a great small town to enjoy a nice dinner, have a craft brew, or stop in one of the many unique craft shops.

After eating and taking a stroll through one of the towns, onto my favorite places to...

Beach it!

  • Belmar, NJ - gets a bad rap sometimes because of the "guidos" and "bennies", but it's a short(er) drive from most places in the state and boasts a nice boardwalk and solid eats on the main drag
  • West Coast, FL
    • - Okay so not a single place, but the west coast of Florida has some great beaches and great weather. Beaching it is great because the water is always warm and calm.
  • Long Beach, NJ - Big beaches with mixed-use nearby for planty of options for dining, drinks, or shopping. Parking can be tough and be prepared to pay for it.
